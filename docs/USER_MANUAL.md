# AURORA User Manual
**Paperless Academic Defense Workflow System**  
**Partido State University (Goa Campus)**

---

## 🎓 1. Student User Manual

### 1.1 Project Registration
1. Navigate to the Login screen and enter your authenticated student email (e.g. `student1@parsu.edu.ph`) and password (`password123`).
2. Click **Login** and you will be directed to the Student Dashboard.
3. If this is a new semester, select **Create Project** on your dashboard.
4. Input your capstone title, select your assigned academic department (e.g. BSIT), list keywords, and select your assigned Research Adviser.
5. Click **Submit** to register your topic.

### 1.2 Document Upload & Versioning
1. Once your project is approved, you will see a detailed defense stepper at the top of your dashboard.
2. Click **Submissions** tab or navigate to the stage card (e.g. Concept Stage).
3. Select **Upload Manuscript** to drag and drop your proposal PDF file.
4. Once uploaded, AURORA will compile it under **Version 1** automatically. When revisions are requested, follow the same upload process; the system will archive previous files and increment to **Version 2**.

### 1.3 Reviewing Annotations & Comments
1. Under the **Revision History** tab, you can view the complete list of comments added by your adviser and panel members.
2. Click any comment to open the split-screen PDF reader and zoom into the matching coordinate highlight on the page.

## 👔 2. Adviser User Manual

### 2.1 Advisee Monitoring
1. Log in as an Adviser (e.g. `adviser1@parsu.edu.ph`).
2. Under the **Guidance Directory** tab, view your list of assigned students and their current stages.
3. View the **Manuscripts Review** tab to see which students have uploaded files awaiting evaluation.

### 2.2 Adding Annotations
1. Click **Evaluate** next to any student manuscript in your queue.
2. The Split-Screen Workspace will load. On the right, read the PDF. Highlighting text or clicking a page triggers the annotation dialog.
3. Select an issue category (e.g., Grammar, Methodology), type your concern, and click **Submit Annotation**.

---

## 🖋️ 3. Panelist User Manual

### 3.1 Evaluating Rubrics
1. Log in as a Panelist (e.g. `panelist1@parsu.edu.ph`).
2. Open the **Assigned Defenses** tab to view your defense calendar timeslots.
3. Click **Evaluate** to open the workspace.
4. On the left side of the split-screen workspace, open the **Grades** panel.
5. Rate each criterion from 0 to 100 based on the active rubric template. The total weighted score is calculated in real time.

### 3.2 Digital Signing
1. Scroll to the bottom of the grading panel, verify your scores, and enter your verification signature name.
2. Click **Submit Evaluation**. AURORA will digitally seal the grading record, locking it to read-only status and generating a unique **Verification Certificate Serial**.

---

## ✉️ 4. Coordinator User Manual

### 4.1 Scheduling Defenses
1. Log in as a Coordinator (e.g. `coordinator1@parsu.edu.ph`).
2. Navigate to defenses schedule page (`/dashboard/defenses/schedule`).
3. Fill out the defense slot: Project, Stage, Date, Time, Room, and Panelists.
4. AURORA will automatically execute a conflict check. If the assigned panelists or rooms are booked in other slots, an alert banner will block submission.
